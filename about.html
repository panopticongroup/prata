About Globe Life American Income Division Prata Organization

The Prata Organization is proud to represent Globe Life’s American Income Division, providing life and supplemental health insurance to individuals, families, and unions.

We believe in flexibility and work-life balance, offering a virtual work environment that empower our agents to design schedules that fit their lifestyles. This approach allows our team to maintain successful careers without compromising personal commitments. We offer flexible, remote opportunities that empower our agents to grow professionally while maintaining work-life balance. Driven by integrity and purpose, we’re committed to making a lasting impact—for our clients, our team, and the communities we serve.


Our actions today have a lasting impact on tomorrow. Therefore, we aim to inspire everyone in our network to help Make Tomorrow Better.

At Globe Life American Income Division Prata Organization, we empower our customers by offering products that help secure their financial future and protect them from the unexpected. We offer sales agents the opportunity to protect more lives and, in doing so, leave a legacy. We give our employees the chance to give back, support initiatives with a service-first mindset, and promote from within.
